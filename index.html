Luca Fresta

I am postdoctoral researcher at Roma Tre University, supported by a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Fellowship. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Bonn, hosted by Prof. Stefan Müller, and an SNSF fellow working with Prof. Massimiliano Gubinelli. I received my Ph.D. in 2020 from the University of Zurich under the supervision of Prof. Marcello Porta. Here you can find my CV.

Research

I do research in Mathematical Physics, with focus on many-fermion systems and Euclidean quantum field theory. I particularly like to approach "old problems" with non-conventional techniques. Topics I have been working on include:

  • Effective dynamics for many-fermion systems
  • Non-commutative stochastic analysis
  • Random operators
  • Transport in 2d lattice fermionic systems
