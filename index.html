Luca Fresta

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Bonn hosted by Prof. Stefan Müller. Previously, I was an SNSF fellow at the University of Bonn and worked with Prof. Massimiliano Gubinelli. I received my Ph.D. in 2020 from the University of Zurich, under the supervision of Prof. Marcello Porta. Here you can find my CV.

Research

My research interests include stochastic analysis, singular stochastic partial differential equations, and mathematical statistical physics, with a focus on supersymmetric methods and probabilistic representations.
