Research

Research Interests

I do research in Mathematical Physics, with focus on fermionic systems and Euclidean quantum field theory. I particularly like to approach "old problems" with non-conventional techniques. Topics I have been working on include:

  • Effective dynamics for many-fermion systems
  • Euclidean quantum field theories
  • Non-commutative stochastic analysis
  • Random Schrödinger operators, random band matrices
  • Transport in 2d lattice fermionic systems
